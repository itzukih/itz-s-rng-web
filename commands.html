Slash commands

Command Reference

All commands use Discord slash syntax. Type / and start typing the command name.

  • Spinning
  • Inventory
  • Information
  • Admin
🎲

Spinning

/spin
Spin once for a random aura. Results are instant with full rarity details.
/spin amount:[1–10]
Spin multiple times at once. Max 10 spins per command. Shows a summary with your best result.
/daily
Claim your daily reward. Grants a x1.5 luck multiplier that boosts your odds on Legendary+ auras. Resets every 24 hours.
/luck
View your current luck multiplier. Luck affects tier 5 (Legendary) auras and above.
🎒

Inventory

/inventory
View your aura collection, sorted rarest first. Shows quantities and your equipped aura.
/inventory user:[@user]
View another player's inventory and equipped aura.
/equip aura:[name]
Equip an aura from your inventory. Your Discord role is automatically set to >> AuraName <<. If you had another aura role, it's swapped out instantly. Supports autocomplete.
/unequip
Remove your equipped aura and delete your aura role from Discord.
📋

Information

/profile
View your player profile: total spins, unique auras collected, luck multiplier, equipped aura and your rarest find.
/profile user:[@user]
View another player's profile.
/auras
List all 19 available auras grouped by rarity, with drop chances shown.
/aura_info aura:[name]
Detailed info for a specific aura: description, rarity, chance, tier and Discord role name. Supports autocomplete.
/leaderboard
Top 10 players in the server ranked by total spins.
/stats
Server-wide statistics: total players, total spins and the 5 most-rolled auras.
/help
In-Discord command list with quick descriptions.
⚙️

Admin Manage Server

Requires the Manage Server Discord permission.

/admin give_aura user aura [amount]
Add an aura directly to a user's inventory. Amount defaults to 1.
/admin remove_aura user aura
Remove an aura from a user's inventory.
/admin set_luck user multiplier
Set a user's luck multiplier (0.1 – 10.0). Affects Legendary+ aura rates.
/admin reset user
Wipe all data for a user: inventory, spin count, luck and equipped aura. Removes their aura role too.
/admin create_roles
Create all 19 aura roles (>> Name <<) in one go. Skips any that already exist. Run this on first setup.
