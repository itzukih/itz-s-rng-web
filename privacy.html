Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026 Itz's RNG Discord Bot © 2026 ItzDev
Your privacy matters. This policy explains exactly what data Itz's RNG collects, why it collects it, and how it is stored. We collect the minimum data necessary to make the bot work.

1. Who We Are

Itz's RNG is a Discord bot developed and operated by ItzDev. It is a free, fan-made hobby project. "We", "us" and "our" refer to ItzDev throughout this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, the following data is stored in our database:

Data Why we store it How long
Discord User ID To identify your account across commands and servers Until you request deletion
Discord Server (Guild) ID To keep your data separate per server Until you request deletion
Aura inventory To track which auras you own Until you request deletion
Total spin count To power the leaderboard and your profile Until you request deletion
Equipped aura To assign and manage your Discord role Until you request deletion
Luck multiplier To apply your luck bonus to spins Until you request deletion
Last spin / daily timestamps To enforce cooldowns fairly Until you request deletion
Spin history log To calculate server-wide stats and most-rolled auras Until you request deletion

We do not collect or store your Discord username, display name, email address, IP address, or any message content.

3. How We Use Your Data

The data listed above is used exclusively to provide the Bot's functionality:

  • Displaying your inventory, profile and leaderboard position
  • Assigning and swapping your aura Discord role
  • Enforcing spin and daily cooldowns
  • Generating anonymous server-wide statistics

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in an SQLite database on the server where the Bot is hosted (Railway). The database is not publicly accessible. We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data, but no system is 100% secure — use the Bot at your own discretion.

5. Data Shared Publicly

The following information is visible to other users within the same Discord server:

  • Your leaderboard rank and total spin count (via /leaderboard)
  • Your aura inventory (via /inventory @you)
  • Your equipped aura and profile stats (via /profile @you)
  • Your equipped aura Discord role (visible in your member profile)

This data is only visible to members of servers where you have actively used the Bot.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of all data the Bot holds about you at any time. To do so:

  • A server admin can run /admin reset @you to delete your data from that server.
  • You can contact us directly (see Section 9) to request a full deletion across all servers.

Upon deletion, all records associated with your Discord User ID are permanently removed from the database and cannot be recovered.

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Bot, please contact us and we will delete their data promptly.

8. Third-Party Services

The Bot runs on Railway (hosting) and is subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy for all interactions that occur on the Discord platform. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third parties.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, you can reach us through our Discord support server or by opening an issue on our GitHub repository. We will respond as soon as possible.
